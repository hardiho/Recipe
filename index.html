Sandwich Receipe

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Tomato
  • Mozzarella
  • Lettuce

Cooking instructions

  1. Put the bread on a plate
  2. Spread butter on the bread
  3. Place cheese on top of the butter
  4. Place tomato on top of the cheese
  5. Place mozzarella on top of the tomato
  6. And finally place lettuce on top of the lettuce
